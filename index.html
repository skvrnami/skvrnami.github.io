Life & death of minor parties in CEE

What happened with the candidates of (minor) political parties? Did they stop running when a minor failed to get a mandate/stop its activities?
Here is some evidence of candidates who turned their coats between two consecutive parliamentary elections.The data were collected using a script comparing candidates' names on the lists controlling for their age. Some caution is advised as there could be some false positives (people of the same name and age) and false negatives (people who changed their name, e.g. women who married).
